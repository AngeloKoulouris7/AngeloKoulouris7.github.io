Angelo Koulouris

Aspiring Writer | Theatre Kid | Film Nerd

Angelo Koulouris (he/him) is an aspiring writer living in Melbourne currently studying a Bachelor of Arts in Creative Writing at RMIT. His interests include watching films and writing screenplays, with a background in youth theatre acting.

He is currently working on a E-Literature project using Twine, creating a non-linear story following a film review website. Additionally, he is in the process of writing a film annotation on The Spirit of the Beehive (1973), with hopes of having it published to Senses of Cinema. He has also published an experimental personal essay with the Bowen Street Press.
